Support

Need help with Tickle? Send details about the issue, your device, operating system version, and the app version if available.

Email support

Data and backups

Use export to create a backup file of counters and logs. Keep important backups somewhere you control before deleting the app or switching devices.

Privacy questions

Tickle is built to work locally for core features. Read the privacy policy for current data handling details.

View privacy policy

Bug reports

Include what you expected, what happened, and steps that reproduce the problem. Screenshots are helpful when they do not include sensitive information.

Feature requests

Tell us what you are trying to count and where the current workflow slows you down. Practical examples help shape future updates.
